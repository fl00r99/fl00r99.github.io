does information want to be free?

The concept that “information wants to be free” is a widely debated idea in the field of information technology. On the one hand, some people argue that information should be freely available and accessible to all, as it is a fundamental human right. Others believe that information should be protected and controlled, as it has economic value and can be used for both good and evil. Ultimately, the answer to this question depends on one’s perspective and values. Some may believe that information wants to be free, while others may not.
